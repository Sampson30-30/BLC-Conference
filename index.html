About the Conference

A day built for FE's digital innovators

Join FE colleagues from across the UK for a day of collaboration, innovation and shared practice. BLC Co‑Lab: Digital Together brings practitioners, digital leads and curriculum teams together to explore what's working, what's emerging, and what's possible.

Expect practical ideas, sector insights and inspiring conversations — all designed to help FE communities learn, share and grow.
